The problem

Canadian investing is quietly expensive.

Every US dividend-paying stock you hold in your TFSA silently loses 15% of every dividend to the IRS — money you can never recover. Most portfolio apps never tell you this.

Add in two accounts with opposite tax rules, a DCA schedule to plan, and a market environment that changes every quarter — and you're left managing all of it in a spreadsheet.

Common questions

FAQ

Do I need to link my brokerage account? ›
No. This app never connects to any brokerage. You enter your holdings manually, import a Wealthsimple CSV, or use the broker import feature — but no OAuth, no API keys to Questrade or RBC, nothing. Your portfolio data never leaves your device.
Do I need an Anthropic API key? ›
No — the Pro plan includes AI access. All AI features (Market Pulse refresh, AI Target Suggestions, AI Diversification Analysis, AI Options Analysis, and Broker CSV Import) are powered by our secure backend proxy. You never need to sign up for Anthropic or manage API keys. AI costs are included in your $99 CAD/year Pro plan. A fair-use limit of 10 AI calls per day applies (resets midnight UTC) — typical usage is 2–3 calls per day. The Basic plan ($49 CAD/yr) includes all portfolio tools but no AI features.
What happens if I clear my browser cache? ›
Your data is automatically synced to a private cloud snapshot — so clearing your browser cache is no longer a data-loss event. Just re-enter your license key and your full portfolio loads from the cloud instantly. Manual JSON backups via the Backup button are still available as an extra safety net.
Can I use this on multiple devices? ›
Yes — your license allows activation on up to 3 devices, and your data syncs automatically across all of them. Open the app on any device, enter your license key, and your full portfolio loads from your private cloud snapshot within seconds. No manual export or import needed. Your cloud data is tied to your buyer account — not to a specific license key — so upgrading plans or getting a replacement key never loses your data.
Does this work for Questrade, RBC DI, or IBKR accounts? ›
Yes. Any broker that exports a CSV with ticker, quantity, and market value columns works with the import feature. The AI import is specifically trained on Wealthsimple's CSV format but can parse most standard exports. You can also always enter positions manually — it takes 5 minutes for a typical portfolio.
Will I get updates after purchasing? ›
Yes. All updates — including new features added throughout the year — are included in your annual subscription. Your plan renews each year at $99 CAD. You can cancel anytime before the renewal date, and the app updates automatically when a new version is deployed to Vercel or GitHub Pages.
Is my financial data safe? ›
Your portfolio is stored in two places you control: browser localStorage and your own private cloud blob. The cloud blob path is a SHA-256 hash of your Lemon Squeezy customer ID — not guessable, not enumerable. Every read and write requires a valid license key (validated against Lemon Squeezy in real time) and a secret server token. The blob URL itself is private — it cannot be accessed without the token even if someone discovered the URL. When you trigger an AI feature, only ticker symbols and position values travel through our proxy to Claude — no account numbers, SINs, or personal information are ever sent.
