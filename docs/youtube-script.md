**[Screen: Split-screen — TFSA vs RRSP tax rules table from the app docs]**

**VO:**
> "Before we dive into the app, let's understand why Canadian investing is uniquely complicated. You have two accounts with opposite tax rules."

**[Zoom into WHT math]**

**VO:**
> "In a TFSA, US dividends lose 15% to IRS withholding tax — permanently. The Canada-US Tax Treaty exempts RRSP accounts, so the same stock in your RRSP pays zero withholding. That's the single most important decision you can make as a Canadian investor: which security goes in which account."

**[Cut to: Dashboard showing sector gap detection]**

**VO:**
